About me

I am a researcher in cloud computing, virtualization and container technologies, and open source community contributor. My research interests revolve around distributed and highly concurrent systems, large cloud infrastructures, networking and SDN, and security.

Currently working as the Security Lead at Tetrate and building a Service Mesh platform for the hybrid and multi-cloud world.

I am a Member of the Apache Software Foundation, where I help with community outreach and the promotion of Open Source and The Apache Way, and committer and member of the Incubator, Community Development, and Apache jclouds project management committees.
